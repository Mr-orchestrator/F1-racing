Paddock Club Experience

The ultimate Formula 1 hospitality experience

Enjoy unparalleled access to the heart of Formula 1. The Paddock Club offers exclusive hospitality suites overlooking the pit lane, gourmet dining, open bars, and unique behind-the-scenes access that brings you closer to the action than ever before.

What's Included:

  • Exclusive pit lane walks
  • Team garage tours
  • Driver appearances and Q&A sessions
  • Gourmet dining with premium wines
  • Open bar throughout the event
  • Prime viewing position above pit lane
  • Official race programme and gift
Starting from $4,500 per person / race weekend

Grid Walk Experience

Walk among the cars and drivers before the race

Experience the electric atmosphere of the starting grid moments before lights out. Walk alongside the cars, see the drivers up close, and feel the tension and excitement that only those on the grid can truly appreciate.

What's Included:

  • Pre-race grid access (30 minutes)
  • Photo opportunities with cars
  • Premium grandstand seating
  • Hospitality lunch
  • Commemorative lanyard and pass
Starting from $1,200 per person / race day

Hot Laps Experience

Feel the speed as a passenger

Strap in for the ride of your life. Experience the circuit from the passenger seat of a high-performance vehicle driven by a professional racing driver. Feel the G-forces, hear the engine roar, and understand what it takes to push a car to its limits.

What's Included:

  • 3 hot laps with professional driver
  • In-car video recording
  • Racing suit and helmet provided
  • Certificate of completion
  • Paddock access
Starting from $800 per person

Team Factory Tour

Go behind the scenes at an F1 team HQ

Get exclusive access to the inner workings of a Formula 1 team. Tour the factory floor, see where the cars are designed and built, visit the trophy room, and learn about the incredible engineering that goes into creating a championship-winning machine.

What's Included:

  • Guided factory tour (3 hours)
  • Design office and wind tunnel viewing
  • Trophy room access
  • Lunch at team facility
  • Team merchandise gift pack
Starting from $350 per person
